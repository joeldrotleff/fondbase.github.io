Section Mix Dry Ingredients
In a large bowl, whisk together 2 cups flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 4 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp fine salt.  :a, 2017-01-01, 5d
Section Mix Wet Ingredients
In a second bowl, whisk together 2 cups of buttermilk, 2 eggs, 4 tbsp melted butter, 1 tsp vanilla extract.  :b, after a, 5d
Combine dry and wet ingredients, whisking until just combined, some lumps are ok  :c, after b, 5d
Let batter rest for 10-15 minutes :d, after c, 15d
Section Cook the Pancakes
Heat a skillet over medium heat and melt one tbsp butter :e, after d, 2d
Working in batches, carefully add a ladleful of batter into skillet and cook until bubbles have formed on the top and the bottom is golden brown. :f, after e, 3d
Flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.  :g, after f, 3d
Top with maple syrup, butter, whipped cream and/or fresh fruit. :h, after g, 1d
